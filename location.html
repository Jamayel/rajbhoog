LOCATION

Rajbhoog Restaurant is located in the heart of the leafy picturesque village of Sunninghill (Ascot) and has been its home since 1986. The name Sunninghill means ‘the home of Sunna's people’, that is the ‘Anglo-Saxon Sunningas tribe’. The restaurant has recently undergone a major refurbishment; adding a contemporary-chic nuance to this distinguished dining venue.
